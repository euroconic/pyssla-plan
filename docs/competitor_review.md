## 🕹️ Deep Dive: The "Big 3" to Beat

### 1. Hama Universe (The "Official" Game)
*   **Vibe:** Highly polished, 3D animated "Islands".
*   **Gap:** It's too "gamey". You can't just open it and design a Minecraft sword quickly.
*   **Our Move:** Be the "Blank Canvas" that Hama isn't.

### 2. Perlypop (The "Pro" Tool)
*   **Vibe:** Photoshop for beads.
*   **Gap:** Apple ecosystem only and costs money.
*   **Our Move:** Be the "Free Perlypop for Everyone".

### 3. Beads Creator (The "Mobile King")
*   **Vibe:** Functional but ad-heavy.
*   **Gap:** Small screen real estate makes 29x29 boards hard to edit.
*   **Our Move:** Tablet/Desktop web view is superior for large boards.

## 💡 Inspiration for Future Features
Based on this review, we should consider:
1.  **"Bead Counter" / Shopping List:** (Like Perlypop) "You need 45 Red, 12 Blue."
2.  **"Virtual Ironing" Animation:** (Like Hama) A fun "Finish" button that melts the visual for a satisfying end-of-task reward.
3.  **Community Gallery:** Since Hama/Perlypop are local-only tools, a **Supabase-powered Cloud Library** (our next phase) would be a massive differentiator.
